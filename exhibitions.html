"Legends of the West: A Portfolio Celebrating America's Wild Mustangs
and Their Rich History."

"Legends of the West" is not just a book; it's a visual journey into the heart of America's most iconic symbol of freedom and untamed spirit - the Wild Mustang. Designed as a 98 page high-quality thick paper, coffee table book, each page is adorned with my personally crafted fine art images that capture the raw, unbridled beauty of these majestic creatures.
