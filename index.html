Wild Mustang Series

Venture into the untamed beauty of the American wilderness with this exclusive Wild Mustang
Series. Each piece in this collection captures the raw energy, majestic grace, and enduring spirit
of these iconic creatures as they traverse their native landscapes. 

Matt Dusig

As a seasoned entrepreneur and former tech CEO, I bring a unique blend of creativity and strategic insight to the world of fine art photography. An Art Major in college, I have now transitioned into capturing the majesty of wild American Mustangs, the serene grandeur of Hawaii’s aerial landscapes, and the dynamic cityscapes of Los Angeles through my lens.

My skills extend beyond traditional photography, encompassing graphic design and a deep understanding of the aesthetic and technical aspects of fine art.
